Music-Enhypen

Enhypen is one of my favorite Kpop boy group from South Korea. I first got to know them since 2023 after listening to one of their songs, "Pass The Mic". Enhypen consists of 7 members who were formed from a survival show called Iland. The names are Niki, Jungwon, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jake, Heeseung, and Jay, listed from left to right. So far they have released 24 total albums including studio albums, EPs, and repackage editions, as well as 9 singles.

I like them because they are very talented with their singing, dancing, songwriting, and music production skills. Not only that, the reason I like them so much is because of their personalities. They are kind, cheerful, funny, and they always care about each other.

Photography

I've always loved taking pictures because it allows me to capture the little moments that make life special. Whether it's a beautiful sunset, a candid smile, or just something that catches my eye, I enjoy freezing that moment in time so I can look back and feel the same emotions all over again. For me, photography isn't just about snapping a photo, it's about telling a story through what I see. I love how each picture holds a memory, and sometimes even a mood, that words can't always describe.

Flowers

I really love flowers. They're so beautiful, calming, and full of color. Every time I see a nice flower, I just feel the urge to take a picture of it. Capturing flowers through my camera makes me feel peaceful and happy, like I'm saving a little piece of beauty to look at whenever I want. Taking pictures of flowers makes me appreciate nature even more, and it's one of the simple things that truly brings me joy. It also helps me express my feelings in a quiet and creative way.

Cats

I have a deep love for cats that goes beyond just being a pet owner. They are fascinating creatures full of personality and charm. Capturing their unique expressions and playful moments through photography has become one of my favorite hobbies. Each picture tells a story, whether it's a curious glance, a lazy stretch, or a mischievous pounce. Taking photos of cats allows me to appreciate their beauty and quirks in a way that feels both creative and deeply personal. It's a joyful way to celebrate the special bond I share with these furry companions.
