My Diploma

Currently I am continuing my studies in the Diploma in Library Informatics at UiTM Kedah and now I am in the fourth semester. I started my studies here in September 2023. So I have studied here for a year and a half. I have faced various things from happy, sad, stressful and various study pressures I have faced. For me, I am very happy to be able to study this course because from an ordinary book I can learn various new things about books. It's not just a book, now I know how the books are managed in the library and how the information in the books is stored.

Apart from subjects related to library materials, I have also studied various other subjects such as technology in libraries, information skills and management, data management, communication skills, metadata development, web content development, programming and also multimedia and digital publishing. I also has learned the cataloging for library materials using cataloging standard such as AACR2R, MARC and RDA. While this semester i learn LCSH, SLSH, LCC and DDC. Previous semester i also has learned the department in the library such as acquistion, circulation, reference services, and many more.
