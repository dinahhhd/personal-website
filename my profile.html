My Favourite Moment in My Life

I think I'm lucky to have a good coworkers that always encourage and support me. What I really like about working here is that I don't have any work pressure, the working hours are quite convenient, meals for staff are provided, there are no bad colleagues, and I have the opportunity to engage in various tasks besides being a waiter.

Overall, I am very happy because my time at Secret Recipe marks my first job experience in my life 💖.
